transcendence ("we," "our," "us") operates a web application that allows users to play the Pong game and interact with other users. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us at:

  • Email: sgodin@student.s19.be

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register on our platform:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Username
  • Email Address
  • Password (encrypted for security)
  • Avatar (optional upload)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to provide and improve the services on our platform, including allowing you to play games, interact with other users, and manage your account.

  • Registration and Account Management: We use your name, username, and email to create and manage your account.
  • Avatar Upload: If you upload an avatar, it will be stored in our database and displayed on your profile and in games.
  • Gameplay: We store your gameplay information, such as win/loss history, scores, and other game statistics. No personal information is stored in game historyâ€”only gameplay details like your score and results.
  • Private Messaging: You can communicate with other users via private chat. Messages are stored in our database to facilitate conversations, but they are deleted permanently when you delete your account.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion

Account Deletion: You have the right to delete your account at any time. When you delete your account, we will:

  • Permanently erase your personal data (name, last name, email, avatar, and messages).
  • Anonymize your gameplay history by replacing your username with "Deleted User" or similar, so that game statistics like scores remain but are no longer associated with you.
  • Remove all private messages you have exchanged with other users.

Once deleted, this action is irreversible, and we will not be able to recover your account or any personal data.

5. How We Store and Protect Your Data

We take security seriously and have implemented measures to protect your personal data. Your password is encrypted, and all other personal data is stored securely in our database. We regularly update our security practices to ensure your data is safe.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The only cookies we use are for purposes such as storing tokens and user IDs.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can request the deletion of your personal data, and we will comply, as explained above.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask us to stop processing your data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If we process your data based on consent (such as for optional features), you can withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, you can contact us at sgodin@student.s19.be.
